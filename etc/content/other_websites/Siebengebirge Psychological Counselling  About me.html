About me... Michael W. Peyton.

I am a fifty-year old Australian who moved to Bad Honnef, Germany, in 2008 to live with my partner.

After initially training as a Structural Engineer, I later enjoyed a fulfilling career in the book industry in Australia, before expanding my skills base in teaching, counselling and psychology.

I have a Masters in Psychological Counselling (equivalent to Diplom Psychologe) and I am a member of the British Psychological Society.

I am presently undertaking training to become a Jungian psychoanalyst at the C.G. Jung Institute in Kusnächt, Zurich.

I have two adult children and regularly return to Melbourne, Australia.
